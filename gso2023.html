Gothenburg Squash Open 2023

October 13-15 2023

Welcome to Gothenburg Open 2023

Draws and entries are available on the tournament software site

Events

  • Men PSA, Men A, Men B, Men C, Motion
  • Women PSA, Women A, Motion

Playing times

Be prepared to play 19.00 on friday

  • Friday 19.00 - 21.30
  • Saturday 9.00 - 19.00
  • Sunday 9.00 - 15.00

Ball

  • PSA -> Dunlop XX
  • All other events -> Tecnifibre XX

Prize money

1000 USD Mens PSA, 1000 USD Womens PSA. Winner receives 500 USD, runner up 250 USD, 3rd place 150 USD och 4th 100 USD

Register

PSA licensed players

Entry fee 300 SEK and can be payed at the venue upon arrival

Non-PSA players

Single event 400 SEK, two events 600 SEK

Payment

Swedish residents can pay by swish to 123 523 19 98 or Bankgiro 5162-0300 Registration fee must be payed on or before October 8. Non-residents can pay upon arrival at the venue.

Venue

The tournament will be held at Landala Squash in the center of Gothenburg. There are several hotels and restaurants at walking distance from the venue.

PSA players can book practice courts free of charge monday - friday before 16.00, call the venue to make a reservation or take a chance and just drop in.

Party

Join us on Saturday evening for a meal at the venue 200 SEK, one beverage included, additional beverages available for purchase. Menu will be available later.
